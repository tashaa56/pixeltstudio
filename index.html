Fitness Tips

Pro Tips

💧

Hydrate Aggressively

Drink at least 3–4 litres of water daily. Even mild dehydration reduces strength output by up to 15%. Sip water throughout your session, not just after.

😴

Sleep is Your Superpower

Muscle is built during sleep, not in the gym. Aim for 7–9 hours. Poor sleep elevates cortisol, promotes fat storage, and tanks testosterone.

🥩

Prioritize Protein

Target 1.6–2.2g of protein per kg of bodyweight daily. Spread it across 4–5 meals. Chicken, eggs, fish, and cottage cheese are your best allies.

📈

Track Progressive Overload

Add weight, reps, or sets each week. Your muscles adapt fast — if the workout feels easy, it's not working. Consistent progression is the secret to results.
